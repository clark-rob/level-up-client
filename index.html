Welcome to Level Up!

Find yourself losing track of all the videogames you own? You have come to the right place, my friend!

If you are new to the site, all you have to do is sign up above*, then sign in to begin logging your collection!

    Once in:
  • Click on the green 'Create Game' button and fill in the fields
  • Once you create, your library begins!
  • You are able to 'Update' and 'Delete' right off each individual game
  • Search for a specific game by ID or Name by clicking thee yellow 'Search Game' Button
  • Now enjoy your easy to access library to help you manage your VIDEOGAMES!
